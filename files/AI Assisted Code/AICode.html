AI Assisted Coding Assignment

Connections Game Project

Introduction

In this assignment, you will create your own version of the Connections game inspired by the New York Times.

The goal is to group words that share a common theme.

How to Play

Find words that match or belong together.

Select FOUR words that form a group.

Click submit to check your answer.

You are only allowed 4 mistakes.

You win by finding all groups before running out of mistakes.

Example

Words like Tassel, Gown, Cap, Diploma all belong to the category Graduation Gear.

AI Chatbot Options

You can use any AI chatbot to help build your project:

• ChatGPT

• Gemini

• Copilot

Tips

Be descriptive when asking AI for help.

Work in small steps to build your project.

Don’t get discouraged if it takes multiple tries.

Requirements

You must create three files:

• index.html

• style.css

• script.js

You must also write a one-page reflection explaining which AI chatbot you used and how much debugging was required.
